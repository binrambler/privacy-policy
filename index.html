Information Collection and Use

The Application collects minimal information necessary to provide and improve the service. This may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address).

Notifications and Intent Processing

The Application uses system notifications to provide you with timely updates and important information. To enable this feature, the Application requests the POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission. No personal identifiers are stored or shared during the process of delivering these notifications. Interaction with notifications is handled via standard Android Intent mechanisms to ensure secure navigation within the Application.

Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling it. You can also manage or disable notifications at any time through your device's system settings.
